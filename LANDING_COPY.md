## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. Does SpendLens need access to my credit card or bank?**
No. SpendLens is a self-reported audit. You tell us how many seats you have, and we provide the optimization math. We never ask for sensitive financial credentials.

**2. How do you keep my data private?**
The audit page you share is stripped of all PII (no email, no company name). Only the tool counts and savings numbers are visible on the public shareable link.

**3. Is this tool really free?**
Yes. SpendLens is free because it's powered by **Credex**. Our goal is to help you find savings first; if you want to access even deeper discounts through credit pooling, we can help with that later.

**4. Which tools do you support?**
Currently, we audit Cursor, GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, Vercel V0, Perplexity, and OpenAI API usage.

**5. How accurate are the savings estimates?**
Our logic engine is updated weekly with the latest pricing tiers (Business vs. Pro vs. Individual) for every major AI provider. We also factor in "tool overlap" where one tool can effectively replace another.
