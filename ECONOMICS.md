# Unit Economics & Financial Projections — SpendLens

## The Math of a Credex Customer
SpendLens is a lead generation engine for **Credex**, which sells discounted AI credits.

**Customer LTV (Lifetime Value) Reasoning:**
- Average Startup spends $2,000/mo on AI tools (API + Seats).
- Credex provides these tools at a 10-15% discount but takes a 20% margin on the savings or a small platform fee.
- Estimated monthly revenue per customer: **$100**.
- Average retention for credit-based subscriptions: 12 months.
- **LTV = $100 * 12 = $1,200**.

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) Plan
Since our GTM plan is $0 paid budget, our CAC is primarily **Time (Developer/Founder effort)**.
- Posting, DMing, and engaging takes ~20 hours/month.
- At an internal cost of $50/hr, monthly organic marketing spend = $1,000.
- If we acquire 100 users/month, **CAC = $10 per user.**
- **LTV:CAC Ratio = 120:1** (Extremely healthy for an early-stage startup).

## Conversion Funnel Estimates
1. **Audit Completed:** 1,000 users/month.
2. **Email Captured (Lead):** 200 users (20% conversion from Audit).
3. **Credex Consultation Booked:** 20 users (10% conversion from Lead).
4. **Credit Purchase (Customer):** 4 users (20% conversion from Consultation).
- **Overall Audit → Customer Conversion: 0.4%**

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
- **Target Revenue:** $1,000,000 / 12 = **$83,333/mo**.
- **Customers Needed:** At $100/mo revenue, we need **833 active customers**.
- **Funnel Backwards:**
    - At 0.4% Audit → Customer conversion, we need **208,250 audits**.
    - Over 18 months, that is ~11,500 audits/month.
    - **~385 audits per day.**

**Is this realistic?**
Yes. Viral AI tools on Product Hunt and r/SaaS often hit 1,000+ audits in a single day. Maintaining a steady 385/day requires:
1. Strong SEO for keywords like "AI Cost Calculator" or "Cursor vs Copilot cost."
2. Integration as a "standard check" in VC portfolio onboarding (Credex unfair advantage).
3. Viral social proof (users sharing their "Saved $500/mo" screenshots).
